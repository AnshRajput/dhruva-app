## Context

The forces at play: what problem, what constraints (technical, platform, license,
timeline), and why a decision is needed *now*. Keep it factual — no solution here.

## Options

Enumerate the real candidates. For each: one-line description + the tradeoff that
matters. Reject strawmen — every option listed must be one a reasonable engineer
would pick.

- **Option A** — …
- **Option B** — …
- **Option C** — …

## Decision

The choice, stated plainly in one sentence, then the reasoning that beat the
alternatives. Cite evidence (scout reports, benchmarks) by ID where it exists.
Mark unresolved evidence as `PENDING <source>`.

## Consequences

- **Positive:** what this buys us.
- **Negative / accepted cost:** what we give up or take on.
- **Follow-ups:** ADRs, spikes, or tasks this decision creates.
